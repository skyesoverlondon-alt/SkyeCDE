ChromeBoard Helper Setup

Download the helper extension zip, extract it, then load the extracted folder in chrome://extensions. After install, use the separate tutorial to understand what each import action does and which URL belongs in the box.

Download helper ZIPOpen full tutorial

  1. Download chromeboard-managed-bootstrap-extension.zip.
  2. Extract the zip.
  3. Open chrome://extensions.
  4. Turn on Developer mode.
  5. Click Load unpacked.
  6. Select the extracted folder.
  7. Open the extension options and paste your deployed ChromeBoard URL. For most people, this should be your Netlify URL or live custom domain.

Important: the extension is a bridge into ChromeBoard. It sends tabs, bookmarks, apps, or managed policy seed items into your board. It does not magically build desktop items unless your ChromeBoard site handles the import.
