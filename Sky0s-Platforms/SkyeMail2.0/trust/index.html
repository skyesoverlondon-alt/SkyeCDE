Trust Pack

This page summarizes security, privacy, and operational controls for Skye Mail Vault — Business Email Center. It’s designed for procurement reviews, vendor risk assessments, and security questionnaires.

Overview SOC2‑ish Controls Subprocessors Retention & Legal Hold Audit Export Format
Confidentiality
Ciphertext‑at‑rest messaging
Message bodies, subjects, and attachments are stored encrypted. Notifications contain no message content.
Access Controls
SSO + Org RBAC
Org roles (owner/admin/viewer), SAML SSO (SP‑initiated) and SCIM provisioning support.
Monitoring
SIEM export stream
Audit events can be streamed to Splunk HEC or Datadog via an outbox + scheduled drain.
Governance
DLP + Legal Hold
DLP policies can block or quarantine content. Legal holds prevent deletion. eDiscovery exports ciphertext + metadata.
