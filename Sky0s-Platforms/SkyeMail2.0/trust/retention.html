Retention Policy

Retention is policy-driven. Audit logs are immutable during retention and exports are standardized.

Audit Events
Immutable until retain_until; DB triggers prevent update/delete before expiry.
Messages
Soft delete supported; legal hold blocks deletion until released.
Attachments
Encrypted and retained with message lifecycle and holds.
Defaults
Audit: 365 days recommended; messages per customer policy; holds case-defined.
