How privacy works

1) You get a public address page.
2) Senders fetch your public key from the server.
3) Sender encrypts the message in their browser.
4) Server stores ciphertext in Neon.
5) You decrypt locally after unlocking with your Vault Passphrase.

Admin recovery key

If you enable recovery, your private key is additionally encrypted for admin recovery. If you do not enable recovery, admin has no way to read or recover message content.
