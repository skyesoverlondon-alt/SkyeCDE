A business email center that doesn’t leak your business. 🔒✨

Skye Mail Vault is a secure “public inbox” for your brand: anyone can send you a message from your public page, you get an instant email alert, and the full message content stays end-to-end encrypted in your vault. Only you can decrypt it — admin can’t, unless you explicitly enable recovery at signup.

End‑to‑end encrypted
Sender encrypts subject + body in their browser using your public key. Server stores ciphertext only.
Inbox + notification
Neon stores encrypted messages. Resend (email) alerts you instantly with a secure link.
Recovery is opt‑in
At signup you can allow admin recovery. If you don’t, nobody can recover the vault.
