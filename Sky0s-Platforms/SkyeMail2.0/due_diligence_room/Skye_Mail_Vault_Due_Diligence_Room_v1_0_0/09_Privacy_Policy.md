# Privacy Policy — Skye Mail Vault — Business Email Center

Effective date: February 21, 2026  
Vendor: Skyes Over London LC  
Contact: SkyesOverLondonLC@solenterprises.org

## 1) Overview
This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Service.

## 2) Information we collect
Account:
- email, handle, password hash, verification/reset token hashes, login timestamps

Vault:
- public keys, wrapped private key blobs, key versions/rotation state

Messages:
- ciphertext content (subject/body), ciphertext attachments
- plaintext metadata (timestamps, sender email/name if provided, thread identifiers, filenames/types/sizes)

Security:
- hashed-IP rate limiting buckets, logs

Cookies:
- essential auth cookie (HttpOnly) and CSRF cookie

## 3) How we use information
To provide the Service, secure it, prevent abuse, deliver verification/reset/notification emails, and debug reliability issues.

## 4) Sharing
We use subprocessors for hosting, database, and email delivery. We do not sell personal information.

## 5) AI assistance (optional)
If you use AI assistance, you direct the Service to send selected content to the configured AI routing service for processing.

## 6) Retention
We retain data while your account is active and as needed to operate and secure the Service. You may request deletion.

## 7) Security
We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is perfect. Device compromise or weak passphrases can expose decrypted content.

## 8) Your rights
You may request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your information at: SkyesOverLondonLC@solenterprises.org
