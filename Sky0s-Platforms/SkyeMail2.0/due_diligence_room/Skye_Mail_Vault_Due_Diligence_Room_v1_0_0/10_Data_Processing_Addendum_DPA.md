# Data Processing Addendum (DPA) — Skye Mail Vault — Business Email Center

Effective date: February 21, 2026  
Processor: Skyes Over London LC  
Controller: Customer

## 1) Scope
Applies when Customer is a Controller of Personal Data and Skyes Over London LC processes as a Processor to deliver the Service.

## 2) Processing details
Subject matter: encrypted inbox and notifications  
Duration: term of Service use + wind-down  
Data subjects: account users; senders submitting messages  
Data: account identifiers; message metadata; ciphertext content; security buckets/logs  
Purpose: provide, secure, and operate the Service

## 3) Processor obligations
- Process on Customer instructions (use of Service)
- Implement appropriate safeguards
- Limit access to authorized personnel
- Notify Customer of Personal Data breach as required

## 4) Subprocessors
Customer authorizes subprocessors listed in “Subprocessors & Data Map.” Equivalent safeguards apply.

## 5) Assistance
Reasonable assistance for data subject requests, noting that message content is encrypted.

## 6) Deletion
Upon request and subject to law, delete Customer account data within a reasonable time. Customer remains responsible for preserving vault access needed to decrypt content.
